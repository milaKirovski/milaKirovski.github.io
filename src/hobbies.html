My love for water sports

It is not a secret that I am a fan of the ocean, as well as the seas and the lakes. I love to be near water.

Every summer I go paddleboarding in the Ohrid Lake. This sport is very fun and exciting. Standup paddleboarders stand on boards that are floating on the water, and use a paddle to propel themselves through the water. For many beginners in this sport, it looks intimidating at first glance, because it is necessary to maintain balance while standing on top of the board. But with effort and courage, everything is achieved. Of course, you should be prepared for many falls into the water while attempting to stay upright on the platform.
