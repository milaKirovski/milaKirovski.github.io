The Wonderful city of Ohrid

Ohrid – the Macedonian pearl

Ohrid in N.Macedonia is the city where I have spent a third of my childhood. When I was little after the school year would finish my parents would bring me to my grandmother in Ohrid. I have spent most of my summer holidays there with her and my cousins.
It is a very special place for me, because it holds so many memories from my childhood. The city is full of history and mesmerising nature.

I love spending time in nature whenever I can, so Ohrid was just the right place for me to stay during summer. Most of my days there were spent by the beach and playing sports with my cousins and friends from my childhood.
Of course, I love to travel the world and explore more exotic places that bring me joy. But Ohrid is truly a gem that should not be left out from your travel destination list.
